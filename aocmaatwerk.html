AOC Interieur Maatwerk

AOC Interieur Maatwerk provides custom-made furniture for interiors for private and the business market as well. Where possible, only the best / (fsc) approved materials will be used to strive for high sustainability. The furniture they make are high quality, minimalistic and with the focus on precision. So for this logo design I wanted to go really minimalistic and symmetrical. The 3 letters AOC are forming a simple but stylish design.
