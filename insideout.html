Inside Out

Inside Out Video and Marketing, the name explains it all. The owner Danny has a sharp eye for everything thats comes with making videos. The name Inside Out is also the motivation while producing and filming. To show the personal idea from a client on the inside, making it a physical form like a video. Danny wanted something personal, a logo thats really him. The cartoony head is a drawing his brother made when they were very young. Can it be any more personal?
