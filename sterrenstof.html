Label design

Brouwerij Sterrenstof

Brouwerij Sterrenstof is a new brewery in the Netherlands. When compiling the recipes, they look for a suitable combination between the period of the year and the character traits of each zodiac sign. The logo was already there, but the labels/style design are done by me. We went for a sleek appearance in combination with bright colors. The focus is on the zodiac sign thats assigned to the beer.
